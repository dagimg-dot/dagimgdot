It is a modern successor to [Hide Items](https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/6771/hide-items/), and is designed to make your GNOME panel cleaner and quieter.

### Features

- Hide all or choosen items on the right side of the panel
- Auto hide items after a delay
- Sleek animations when showing and hiding items
